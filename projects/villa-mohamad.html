Project 01 — A Villa for a Family

A villa for living,
not just looking.

We don't decorate rooms — we resolve how they feel to walk into.

The brief was a private residence for a Qatari family — generous, calm, and unmistakably theirs. We designed and delivered the interior end-to-end: layout, finishes, joinery, lighting and final styling.

Walnut panelling, book-matched marble bathrooms and custom millwork carry through every floor. Each bedroom holds its own mood — restrained, warm, and built around how the family actually lives in the space.

From concept to keys, the project ran for eight months under a single project manager — typical of how we deliver our turnkey villa work.
