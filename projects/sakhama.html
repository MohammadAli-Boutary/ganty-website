Project 06 — Build & Fitout, Combined

Construction and fitout,
combined.

Shell and fitout under one team, on one schedule.

Sakhama is one of our larger ongoing engagements — a full building shell delivered alongside interior fitout, structural work and MEP coordination.

Curved drywall ceilings, ribbed plasterboard soffits and a circular feature drum at the entrance — all built from drywall framing and finished to a fine surface tolerance before paint.

The project demonstrates how our construction and fitout divisions work together: shell, fitout, and final delivery under one team.
