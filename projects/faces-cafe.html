Project 03 — A Café at The Pearl

A café at The Pearl,
dressed for the evening.

A jewel-box room that holds the noise.

Faces Café needed presence on a busy marina-front strip and an interior that worked equally well at 10am and 10pm. The answer was a dark, jewel-box room.

Lacquered timber, gold trim, deep crimson banquettes and warm pendant lighting — the kind of room you settle into. Detailing on the mouldings, the millwork bar, the pastry display, and the espresso station was developed against shop drawings we produced in-house.

Faces has run since 2013 and remains one of our most-photographed early projects.
